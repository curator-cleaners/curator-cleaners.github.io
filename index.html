Company at a glance

Presentation management for active listings.

Curator exists to make sure a listed property is always ready to impress during viewings, inspections, photo sessions, and other marketing interactions.

Service footprint Nationwide across South Africa
Property types Residential, office, commercial, and selected land
Operating phase Listing and marketing period only
Client model Curator contracts directly with owners
Service model Structured presentation support, not once-off cleaning

The Curator advantage

Value buyers feel before they negotiate.

Curator is a real estate support service built to protect the image, market readiness, and presentation quality of a listed property from the day it enters the market until it is sold or rented.

For property owners

Maintain a clean, organised, market-ready property throughout the active listing period.

For referring agents

Introduce owners to a presentation partner that helps properties show better without billing the agent.

Listing-phase only

Curator operates during the sale or rental marketing period, not as a general cleaning company.

Presentation management

Ongoing cleaning coordination, viewing checks, show-day readiness, and presentation oversight in one flow.
