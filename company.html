Company details

What Curator Property Presentation Co. actually does

Curator helps property owners keep listed properties consistently clean, organised, and presentation-ready nationwide while they are actively on the market for sale or rent.

Nationwide support

Curator operates across South Africa for listed properties that need consistent presentation control.

Listing-phase focus

Curator works during the real estate listing and marketing period only.

Presentation readiness

Support covers viewings, inspections, photo sessions, show days, and other marketing moments.

Property types

Residential, office, commercial, and selected vacant land presentation contexts are supported.

Not general cleaning

This is a structured presentation service, not a general once-off cleaning company.

How the model works

Owners contract directly. Agents may introduce the service.

Curator contracts directly with the property owner, not the real estate agent. Agents are not charged for the service. They may introduce Curator to sellers or landlords whose properties need to remain in top presentation condition during the listing period.

Owner relationship

Curator manages the presentation side directly with the owner during the active listing term.

Agent support

Agents benefit from better-presented properties without becoming the billed service client.

Market protection

The goal is to protect the value, image, and first impression of the property while it is being marketed.

Values

Every listed property should remain polished, cared-for, and ready to impress throughout the active listing period.

Structured support

Curator combines ongoing cleaning coordination, visual upkeep, presentation checks, and readiness management in one service model.
